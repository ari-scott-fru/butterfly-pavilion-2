At the Denver Butterfly Pavilion, we're dedicated to inspiring conservation and connecting people with the incredible world of invertebrates. Our 7,200-square-foot tropical conservatory houses over 1,600 butterflies from around the world.

Beyond butterflies, explore our interactive exhibits featuring fascinating creatures like tarantulas, sea stars, and our beloved Rosie the Chilean rose-hair tarantula.
